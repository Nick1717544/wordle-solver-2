Wordle Solver

Info

The Wordle Solver is pretty simple. Once you enter your word into Wordle enter the information into Wordle Solver.
First you enter the word by typing on your keyboard (Note: Wordle Solver will only accept letters)
You can select if the letter was green, gray, or yellow. You do this by clicking on the letter.
Like most of my projects it is open source, all the source code is on Github.
